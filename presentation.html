Ecosystem Synergy

Scenario: "Buy 3 specific items, Get 20% Off"
1. Define Rule: Merchant sets "Buy 3, Get 20%" in GoKwik Discount Module.
2. Display Overlay: Kwik Discovery shows on eligible items: "Add 2 more for 20% Off!"
3. Guide via Side-Cart: After 2nd item added, side-cart prompts: "Add 1 more for 20% Off!"
4. Fulfill at Checkout: GoKwik Checkout auto-applies 20% discount. No codes needed.
5. Personalize (Optional): Logged-in VIP sees overlay: "VIP: Buy 3, Get 25% Off!"
